Since the dawn of time, the greatest strides forward have been taken when we moved as one. We have always thrived – and survived – on collaboration, communication and creativity. And we always will.


And yet, despite an urgent need for creative solutions to some of the world's most pressing challenges, access to an arts education is increasingly for only the select few. We're creators ourselves and we get how hard it is to develop your artistic skills and break into the creative industry.


Today's art schools are expensive, elitist and tied to physical buildings in just a small number of cities worldwide. Accessing spaces dedicated to true collaboration and communication has never felt more difficult, and creativity is under threat. It's an outdated approach to nurturing creativity and we stand with many others demanding radical transformation. We are fired up to make it easier and better for everyone.

SayHere is founded on the belief that pushing forward together, with a shared vision, is what sparks creativity and drives change. It's our purpose to bring creators together and support them to do amazing work.

 

Regardless of location, background or life stage, SayHere is where creators hang out, learn and kickstart their careers. It's a new route into the creative world; through connections made organically and learning at your own pace. We nurture fresh visions, incredible collaborations, total creativity, and a diverse and positive community.

 

Our mission is to see SayHere creators grow in confidence, take up space in their chosen fields, and build lasting careers. It's our hope that SayHere becomes synonymous with groundbreaking and impactful creativity at all levels, in all fields, and from creators of all kinds.
