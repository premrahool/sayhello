About

U CAN UKE is a YouTube channel that delivers ukulele tutorials and play-along videos each week. Since starting on YouTube in April 2017, UCU has received over 435,000 subscribers and 36 million video views--becoming one of the fastest growing ukulele channels.

 

Aside from YouTube, Matt from UCU teaches at ukulele festivals and performs a few shows per year to thousands of people. He has even opened in concert for Grammy-Award winning artists Switchfoot & Michael W. Smith, performed with Grammy-nominated artist Led Kaapana, and sang at a convention for former U.S. President George W. Bush.

The Session

In this workshop, U CAN UKE will guide you through a few basic fingerpicking patterns as you develop muscle memory to play the ukulele.

 

This session also includes a FREE fingerpicking printable to help enhance your learning experience of this 4-stringed instrument.
